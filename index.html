ABOUT ME

A native of Taiwan, I am a trained lifespan developmental psychologist, a lifelong dancer, and a movement educator based in Fort Collins, Colorado, United States. My program of research focuses on the psychological and motivational processes that promote healthy and optimal adult development and aging. My passion lies in sharing the art and science of movement with the Gyrotonic® and Gyrokinesis® methods for both rehabilitative and recreational purposes.
